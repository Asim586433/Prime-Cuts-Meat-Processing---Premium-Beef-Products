About Prime Cuts

Our commitment to quality and sustainability since 1985

Our Story

Prime Cuts began as a small family farm in 1985 with a commitment to sustainable farming practices and ethical treatment of animals. Today, we've grown into a full-service meat processing business while maintaining our core values.

We partner with local farms that share our commitment to quality and sustainability. All our beef is grass-fed, free-range, and hormone-free.

Our state-of-the-art processing facility is USDA approved, and we follow the highest standards of food safety and quality control.

Our Process

We take pride in our meticulous process that ensures the highest quality meat products:

  • Careful selection of livestock from trusted local farms
  • Humane treatment and stress-free environment for animals
  • Expert butchering by our skilled craftsmen
  • Rigorous quality control at every step
  • Proper aging for enhanced flavor and tenderness
  • Vacuum-sealing for freshness and longer shelf life

This attention to detail results in superior taste and texture that our customers have come to expect from Prime Cuts.

Why Choose Us

Sustainable Farming

We practice regenerative agriculture that improves soil health and reduces environmental impact.

Animal Welfare

Our animals are raised stress-free with plenty of space to roam and natural feed.

Premium Quality

We follow strict quality standards to ensure you receive the best possible products.
